Japan: A Place Close to My Heart

Why Japan is Special to Me

Japan has always been a big part of my identity. My mom is from the Kanagawa prefecture near Tokyo, and I've had the chance to visit often, creating some of my best memories there. It's a place filled with beautiful traditions, stunning landscapes, and a vibrant culture that I love to share.

Discovering Kanagawa and Tokyo

Kanagawa, where my family is from, is close to Tokyo and offers the best of both traditional and modern Japan. From Yokohama’s beautiful waterfront to Tokyo's buzzing city life, there is always something to see and do. I have so many cherished memories here, and it’s my favorite spot to visit.

The Passion of Baseball in Japan

Baseball holds a special place in Japan's heart, and my visit to the Yokohama BayStars stadium was a memorable experience. The atmosphere was electric, with fans showing incredible passion for the game while maintaining an unmistakable sense of politeness and respect. The culture surrounding baseball in Japan is not just about the sport but about community and tradition, something I truly admire and connect with every time I visit.

Looking Forward

My goal is to live in Japan one day. Each visit brings me closer to this dream, and I can’t wait to experience life in a place that feels like home. I’m excited to continue exploring all Japan has to offer!
